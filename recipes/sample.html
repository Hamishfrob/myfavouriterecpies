Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix butter and sugar
  3. Add eggs and flour
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes
